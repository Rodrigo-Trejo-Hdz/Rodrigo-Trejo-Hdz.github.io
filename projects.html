OEE Production Tracker — Bonafont

Streamlit app built for a Bonafont (Danone) production facility that had no structured KPI measurement system. Computes and visualizes Overall Equipment Effectiveness (Availability × Performance × Quality) per shift, with trend analysis against the 85% world-class benchmark — enabling structured reporting for plant management decisions.

Python Streamlit Pandas NumPy
def oee_score(shift): a = availability(shift.hours, shift.downtime) p = performance(shift.output, shift.rated_capacity) q = quality(shift.output, shift.defects) return a * p * q # benchmark: 0.85
