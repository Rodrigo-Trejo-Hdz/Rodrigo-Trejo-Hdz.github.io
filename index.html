About Me

I studied Data Science and Mathematics Engineering at ITESM — a program grounded in applied statistics, optimization, and machine learning that gave me the mathematical foundation to go beyond dashboards and into model-driven solutions.

My day-to-day spans AWS pipelines (Glue, S3, Athena), Power BI dashboards for enterprise clients like FEMSA, Bonafont, and Coppel, and full-stack internal tools with React and PostgreSQL — all applied to retail pricing data across Mexico, the US, and Latin America.

My direction is toward Data Science and ML engineering — applied to pricing, demand forecasting, and product similarity — building systems that are both statistically rigorous and production-ready.
